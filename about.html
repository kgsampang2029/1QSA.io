A Little More About Me!

Hello, Fandom! I am Krizalee Sampang, the author of this page, and I am a huge fan of Sanrio!

I made this website because Sanrio has been a big part of my life since I was a kid. It all started when I got to go to the Hello Kitty Town. Here are things to know about me and my interest in Sanrio and its characters!

Recent Favorite Character Cinnamoroll! He's so cute and I love the color blue
First Favorite Character My Melody! I met My Melody in person at the Sanrio Hello Kitty Town in Malaysia
Favorite Hello Kitty Game Hello Kitty Island adventure! I loved playing exploration games as a kid
Favorite Merch Cinnamoroll Plushie! While there are a lot of Cinnamoroll merch, plushies are definitely the cutest
Favorite Movie/Series My Melody and Kuromi! The first Sanrio series I ever watched on Netflix. It was so cute

I hope this site is able to help you discover more information about the characters!
