About Hello Kitty

Hello Kitty's full name is Kitty White. She's a fictional character from the Japanese company, Sanrio, who made her debut in 1974 and has been their main mascot ever since.

Appearance and Personality

Kitty White is a white Japanese Bobtail cat. She's famously known for her red bow, that represents human connections, on her left ear and her simple design. Unlike most characters, she doesn't have a visible mouth. Her lack of a mouth means we should express ourselves with actions, not just words.

Fun Facts

  • Her design was a huge success and became the face of the "kawaii" community in Japan.
  • She is one of the most globally recognized characters, right up there with Mickey Mouse and Mario.
  • Sanrio's founder, Shintaro Tsuji, created her to show that a small gift or a kind gesture can help you make friends and show appreciation.
