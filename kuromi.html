About Kuromi

Kuromi is a white rabbit who is the self-proclaimed rival of My Melody. She was born on Halloween (October 31st). She made her first appearance in 2005 as an antagonist of Onegai My Melody.

Appearance and Personality

She wears a black jester's hat with a pink skull that changes its expression. She looks tough and punk, but she's actually very girly on the inside. She loves to write in her diary and is a fan of romantic stories.

Fun Facts

  • She's the leader of a biker gang called "Kuromi's 5," but they only ride tricycles.
  • In her first show, she was a villain, but now she's a good friend to Hello Kitty and the others.
  • She is one of Sanrio's most popular characters, especially with fans who like punk and goth styles.
  • Her favorite foods are meat, pickled onions, shallots, and pumpkin-based products.
