About Cinnamoroll

Cinnamoroll's real name is Cinnamon. He was born on March 6th, high up in the clouds. His tail looks just like a cinnamon roll, which is how he got his name.

Appearance and Personality

Cinnamoroll is a puppy with a fluffy white body, bright pink cheeks, and sky-blue eyes. He is shy but very friendly and loves to help his friends. He works at a place called Caf√© Cinnamon, and his favorite food is warm, fresh cinnamon rolls.

Fun Facts

  • He is one of the most popular Sanrio characters, winning first place in the Sanrio Character Ranking from 2020 to 2024.
  • He can fly through the air by flapping his big ears!
  • When he was first created, the Sanrio team worried his blue color wouldn't be popular with fans, but it ended up making him really famous.
  • Sometimes people, especially his friend Mocha, think he's a girl and dress him up in cute bows.
