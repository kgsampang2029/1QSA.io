About My Melody

This rabbit's name is My Melody, frequently shortened as My Melo or Melody. Her birthday is on January 18, and she made her debut around the same time as Hello Kitty did.

Appearance and Personality

My Melody is a white bunny and she wears a pink hood that covers her head. She often has a blue or yellow bow or a white flower on her right ear. She is very cheerful, honest, and tends to be girly. Surprisingly, her dream is to sleep a lot.

Fun Facts

  • My Melody was actually inspired by Little Red Riding Hood! But Sanrio abandoned this role in the mid-1900s.
  • Her hobbies are watching DIY videos and baking almond pound cakes as it is her favorite food!
  • My Melody's name is actually a pun on a sequence of notes and the fact that she sings cute songs.
  • She used to have a rivalry with Kuromi, but eventually became close friends with her.
