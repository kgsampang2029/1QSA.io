About Pochacco

Pochacco is a white beagle dog who was born on February 29th, on a leap year. He debuted in 1989, and his name came from the Japanese word "pocha pocha" which means chubby and cute.

Appearance and Personality

He is a dog with black floppy ears, a black nose, and a round body. Like Hello Kitty, he does not have a visible mouth. He is known for being a very athletic puppy. He is very clumsy and loves going on walks, despite having trouble walking.

Fun Facts

  • He was based off of Snoopy, who is a mascot of the Peanuts franchise! This contributed to Pochacco's popularity.
  • He is 4 cups of banana ice cream tall and weighs 3 obake carrots. But some media showed larger sizes.
  • His favorite food is banana ice cream, and he dreams of his tummy being filled with such a meal.
  • He was the most popular Sanrio character for five consecutive years, from 1991 to 1995.
