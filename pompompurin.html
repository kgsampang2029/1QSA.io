About Pompompurin

Pompompurin, shortened as Pompom or Purin, is a Golden Retriever dog introduced by Sanrio in 1996. He was born on April 16th, and lives in his own basket in the entrance hall of his owner's house.

Appearance and Personality

He is recognizable by his trademark brown beret and his huggable size. He is a laidback and friendly character who loves to make new friends. His hobbies include collecting shoes and doing "purin aerobics."

Fun Facts

  • Pompompurin likes when his owner says "Let's go out!", and dislikes the command "stay!"
  • His favorite food is his mother's homemade pudding. He also loves milk.
  • Pompompurin appears as furniture in the video game Animal Crossing: Pocket Camp.
  • He spends most of his time either napping or going on walks with his best friend, Muffin.
